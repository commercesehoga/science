Frequently Asked Questions
Is this platform completely free?
Yes! Chemistry Hub is 100% free — no paywalls, no subscriptions, no ads. Every note, formula sheet, and mock test is freely accessible to all students. It is powered by Wondermayank and ThunderStudy as a public education initiative.
Which exams does this platform cover?
We cover JEE Mains & Advanced, NEET UG, CUET UG, and CBSE Board exams for Class 11 and Class 12. Our chemistry content is carefully mapped to NCERT with additional topic coverage for competitive exams.
How are the mock tests structured?
All mock tests are in CBT (Computer-Based Test) format — the same interface used in the actual JEE and NEET exams. We offer full-length mocks, chapter-wise tests, and PYQ practice sets with detailed solutions and score analytics.
Are the notes aligned with NCERT?
Absolutely. All notes follow the NCERT syllabus chapter by chapter. For JEE and NEET, we also include additional concepts and questions that go beyond NCERT but are frequently asked in competitive exams.
How is JEE different from NEET chemistry?
JEE chemistry emphasises Physical Chemistry calculations, Organic mechanisms, and numericals in depth. NEET chemistry is more factual, covering NCERT lines and conceptual understanding. Both share most of the same Class 11 & 12 chapters, but with different question styles and difficulty levels.
Can I access this on mobile?
Yes! Chemistry Hub is fully responsive and works on mobile, tablet, and desktop. Use the bottom dock to navigate on mobile. For mock tests, we recommend a desktop or tablet for the best CBT experience.
Who makes this content?
Content is curated and developed by Wondermayank, a student-run education initiative. The platform is part of the ThunderStudy and CommerceSeHoga ecosystem — built by students, for students across India.
